My Culinary Adventures

Experimenting in the Kitchen

One of the exciting new pursuits I've taken up is cooking. Throughout the year, I've experimented with various dishes, and I must say, I've managed to create some pretty fantastic meals. It's been a rewarding experience that has added a flavorful dimension to my life.

Stuffed Bell Pepper Jack O' Lanterns

I made this for Halloween, with ground beef, chili peppers, tomatoes, and other assorted veggies. I cut Jack O' Lantern faces and broiled them in the oven. So yummy!

Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Being from Southern Louisiana, Cajun food was a big part of my diet growing up. This dark Gumbo is the perfect combo of spicy and comforting, and brings back so many happy memories for me.

Accordian Cucumbers with Chili Oil

For this dish, I made a chili oil from scratch and learned how to cut cucumber in a way that makes them almost slinky like.

Chicken Noodle Soup

I first made this soup when my boyfriend caught the Flu. I made my own chicken stock and noodles for the receipe and I can guarentee that it not only heals aches and pains, but the soul as well.

These are the best rolls I've made all year.

Dough

  • 1 cup warm fresh milk [240 ml]
  • 1 whole egg [large]
  • 3 tbsp sugar [38 grams] or honey if preferred
  • 2 tsp instant yeast [6 grams]
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour [390 grams]
  • 1/2 tsp salt [3 grams]
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter [45 grams]

Cinnamon Butter

  • 1/3 cup softened butter [80 grams]
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar [14 grams]
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon powder [2 grams]
  • 1 tbsp honey [15 ml]
